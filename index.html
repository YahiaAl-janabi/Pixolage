PIXOLAGE
The world's most inspiring image viewer.

Local Project Based


Your data is YOUR data!

A local project based workflow helps to navigate your photographs with maximum efficiency. Source photographs are not copied or modified.

Pixolage respects your privacy and does not collect any analytic information, nor communicate any telemetry data what so ever.

FEATURES

Scalability


It can handle HUNDREDS of THOUSANDS of images!

Due to the multi process design of Pixolage, it does not suffer the normal limitations of your usual image viewers.

No need to split collections containing many photographs into multiple directories in order to become manageable.

Performance


It is FAST!

Pixolage had been optimised to use multiple CPUs in order to maximise the use of your CPU during high-load periods. Pixolage provides the fine grain controls to enable you to get the most out of your hardware.

No need to wait around while the software performs photograph indexing.


Tag Browser


Efficiently organise your photographs by tagging them. Tag databases can be exported to plain text.

Support for ICC Colour Profiles and many file formats

Support for industry standard image and photo metadata (IPCT-IIM, EXIF).
Supports ICC Colour profiles.
Supports many standard and RAW camera file formats.

Bookmarks

Save your place within the wall for easy retrieval.
